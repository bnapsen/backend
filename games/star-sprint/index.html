Internet-ready multiplayer chess

Neon Crown Chess

Classic chess with a richer table-style board, one-click room hosting, quick invite links, drag controls on phone and desktop, and six Stockfish 18 solo difficulty levels right in the browser.

1
Enter a name

Use any display name so your opponent sees who joined.

2
Host or join

Hosting creates a room instantly. Joining works by code or shared link.

3
Play proper chess

Castling, en passant, promotion, check, checkmate, and stalemate all work.

Controls

Mouse: click or drag. Phone: drag pieces. Keyboard: arrows move focus, Enter selects, Escape clears.

Sound

Move, capture, check, and game-end cues are enabled.

Invite link

Host first, then copy this link and send it to the other player. They will land in the right room automatically.

Advanced connection settings

Host a match to create an invite link, join with a code from a friend, or play solo against Stockfish.

How to play

Easy remote setup

Fastest host flow

Press Host match, wait for the room to appear, and then copy the invite link.

Fastest join flow

Open the invite link, type a name, and press Join match.

Proper chess rules
