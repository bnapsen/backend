Survival advice

Stay alive longer

Use the rifle to open

The rifle is the most stable hitscan weapon. Use it to thin the first line and swap only when the horde compresses.

Shotgun the choke

Let the shotgun erase anything already in your face. It lands hard but falls off fast.

Protect downed teammates

As long as someone is alive, the room can recover. Sprint to create space and keep the respawn timer alive.
